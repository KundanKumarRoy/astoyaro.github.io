Pricing & Policy

-: Pricing & Privacy :-

At AstroYaro, we always try to charge a reasonable amount for our reliable services. Before hiring our astrologers, you can check our pricing policies.

-: Our average price rates :-

Due to our customized pricing scheme, you will pay for your purchase. The price person can range from Rs500 to Rs 2000 per session per user. Moreover, the type of astrological services you have chosen can also make a difference.

-: Payment schedule:-

You can engage our astrologers for a particular period. Everything will be mentioned in the service description. For instance, some of our customers keep our astrologers engaged for 1 month. You can set the duration based on your needs.

-:Budget-friendly pricing:-

You will not need to go beyond your budget for this purpose. We aim to provide our services at the most competitive rate. Without compromising our professionalism, we offer quality services to our clients.

-:Adjustment of your deals:-

In some cases, the price of the chosen services gets reduced within a week of the registration data. But you will not find that reduced price, as you have already booked our services. While you have booked a preferred slot for astrological services, there is no way of rescheduling it to a different date. It may result in booking cancellation, and so, you must read the cancellation policy of our company.

-: Errors in pricing details:-

Our team always tries to ensure that the pricing details are highly accurate. Still, our given estimate has a minimal risk of pricing errors. When we find the price of our astrological service is higher than what is displayed, we will cancel the booking. You will also receive a notification about it.

These are some basic information about our pricing policies. We have the right to deny the service request of any person who seems to misuse the service.
